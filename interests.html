    Girl Guiding
  • I have been a member of Girlguiding UK since 2000, as a Brownie, Guide, Young leader, Occasional helper, and Unit leader.
  • I helped start a new Young Leader unit for ages 14 to 25 in 2007, and I participated in and helped run several large scale events in Gloucestershire.
  • As an adult, I have recently opened up a Guide unit in my area, as a unit leader. We're currently looking forward to our first pack holiday (camping)!
  • I love the fact that Girlguiding teaches about empowerment, giving girls and young women a safe space to learn, grow, and try new things.
